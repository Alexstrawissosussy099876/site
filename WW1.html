A BRIEF OVERVIEW OF WW1

World war one was fought between the years of 1914 and 1918.

It was probably one of the most brutal wars ever fought.

The weapons and machinary used in the war were so advanced, all it took was a shot and you were dead.

Trenches were one of the key things in WW1

It greatly increased the safety of the soldiers.
